These datasets are from the article:
Optical modeling of wide-bandgap perovskite and perovskite/silicon tandem solar cells using complex refractive indices for arbitrary-bandgap perovskite absorbers,
Optics Express 26 (21), (2018) 27441-27460.

The perovskite compounds that are used are:
-MAPI (Eg of 1.557 eV)
-CsBr (Eg of 1.615 eV and 1.665 eV).
